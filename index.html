About Us

The Buckeye Government Challenge was created to help Ohio students and residents better understand the structure and importance of their state government. Our goal is to make learning about Ohio’s laws, leaders, and history engaging and accessible for everyone.

Through interactive study guides, quizzes, and maps, users can explore how Ohio’s government works, discover key figures in leadership, and test their civic knowledge — all while having fun!

Whether you’re preparing for a class, reviewing for a test, or just curious about your state, the Buckeye Government Challenge gives you the tools to become a more informed and active citizen.
